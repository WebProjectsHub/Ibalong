Volcanic eruptions
Simultaneous eruption of Kolasi, Hantik and Isarog.  The eruption produced an enormous earthquake, it reshaped the land, resulting in the formation of Pasacao and Malbogon.  

The Inarihan River, previously flowing east, changed it course and now flows to the west, to Ponon. 

In Bato, a mountain submerged and became a lake.

The Calabanga gulf disappeared.  It displaced the Dumagats, who then moved to Catmon after the eruption.
